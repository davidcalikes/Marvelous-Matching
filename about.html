Welcome to Marvelous Matching: About Page

Marvelous Matching is a simple matching game designed for infants or children with special needs and/or learning difficulties.

The game allows users to choose from three different matching categories: Animals, Shapes and Colours.

Designed and tested within a special learning environment with professional guidance from teachers and support staff, Marvelous Matching is an ideal classroom and homeschool resource for infants and young people with special needs to help achieve their educational goals.
